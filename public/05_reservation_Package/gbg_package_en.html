Reservation Info

  • 100% pre-booked, and there's no separate on-site sales.
  • Must arrive 10 minutes before the departure time.
  • The reservation for the website opens 2 weeks ago on Tuesday.
  • The reservation will be completed after payment is made.
  • Tickets that have not been sent due to incorrect phone number cannot be refunded, so please fill out the phone number accurately.
  • If you make a reservation through real-time account transfer, it will be automatically canceled if the deposit is not confirmed by midnight on the day of the reservation.
  • In the case of reservations by call for persons aged 65 or older, persons of national merit, and persons with disabilities, evidence must be brought with them, and applicants must attend. Please understand that it will be canceled if you are absent from the site.
  • Cancellation or change is not possible after the deadline.
  • There may be an event that will be canceled in case of rain based on the forecast of the Korea Meteorological Administration at 11 a.m. on the day of the event. Individual notification and full refund will be made.

Cancellation Info

  • In order to establish a proper reservation culture, the refund standard is operated as follows when cancelling the reservation, so please check and make a reservation carefully.
  • You can only visit and use the selected date when making an online reservation, and refunds or changes of dates cannot be made after the date and time have passed.
  • After the online reservation is completed, you cannot change the schedule such as the reservation date and reservation time, and if you want to change it, you must cancel the payment and make a reservation again.
  • If you want to cancel the reservation, you can cancel it from [Reservation -> Reservation Details].
  • Customers arriving after a departure time of more than 30 minutes may be restricted without refund.
  • Cancellation refunds take 3-5 business days.

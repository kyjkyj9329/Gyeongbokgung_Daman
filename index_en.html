  • 2023 Gyeongbokgung Saeng Gwa Bang

    Saeng-gwa-bang, located in the pavilion of So-ju-bang in Gyeongbokgung Palace, is one of the royal court's Yuk-cheup-so, and was also called Saeng-mul-bang or Saeng-geot-bang as a place where desserts and special meals for the King and Queen were prepared. The Gyeongbokgung Saeng-gwa-bang Program is an experience program designed to allow visitors to enjoy royal medicine and tea that the actual king ate today based on the contents of the Annals of the Joseon Dynasty.

    2023.04.20 ~ 2023.06.28

  • [The Old Palace Concert] To add groove to the wind stream

    The Old Palace Concert is a palace-specific performance, and we would like to provide high-quality performance viewing contents by utilizing the location and regional characteristics of the palace. In the first half of 2023, the permanent performance "To add groove to the wind stream" will present a high-quality performance that collaborates variously with Korean traditional music, traditional dance, and jazz.

    2023.05.29 ~ 2023.06.06

  • [Royal Cultural Festival] Humanities Concert

    Jip-ok-chae, which means "collecting precious treasures like jade," is a place where King Gojong used it as a study during the Joseon Dynasty and received foreign envoys. King Gojong read books and had a time of contemplation here. Based on the special value of a library in the palace, it was first opened to the public in 2016, but now it is only open for some periods. Through this opportunity, we will open the Palace Bookstore so that you can look around the quiet space of King Gojong in the beautiful palace.

    2023.06.20 ~ 2023.07.28

  • The Opening Ceremony of the 2023 Royal Culture Festival

    To mark the 40th anniversary of the return of the name of Changgyeonggung Palace, the 9th door of the Royal Cultural Festival will be opened. Please visit the palace with a welcome step and join us at a meaningful place to tell the story of the Palace of Finding Again. Gwanghwamun will be held, and a parade to mark the beginning of the royal cultural festival will enter and present creative performances based on the motif of the palace's miscellaneous statues and Cheoyongmu. Following the chorus with the children's choir and the cast of , the 2023 "New Royal Banquet" will be held to celebrate the opening of the festival.

    2023.07.28 ~ 2023.07.30

  • 2023 Gyeongbokgung Palace: Starlight Tour

    On a star-filled June night, the gate of Gyeongbokgung Palace opens. Under the subtle starlight, let's enjoy a Korean traditional music performance at Gyeongbokgung Soju Room, taste the king's Surasang, and go on a starlight walk at Gyeongbokgung Palace with a professional commentator. Go through Janggo, Jipokjae, Palujeong, and Geoncheonggung Palace to meet the old stories of the palace, and feel the atmosphere of Hyangwonjeong where Gojong walked under the pouring starlight

    2023.06.15 ~ 2023.07.13

  • 2023 Sympathy for Suragan's Tasting

    Tasting Sympathy is a traditional cultural complex experience program that directly experiences court food culture under the theme of seeing, eating, enjoying, and touching court culture. There are royal soldiers sitting in the pavilion, royal food, snacks that can only be tasted in the alleyways of Haengnangchae, and traditional games with courtiers in the Joseon Dynasty.
